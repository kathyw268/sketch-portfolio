About

My name is Kathy Wu. I'm currently based in Philly where I study Marketing and Interactive Design at Drexel University. I love making something out of nothing; specifically, clean, simple, user-friendly web pages.

As silly as it sounds, my interest in web design first sparked when I was 12 years old from Neopets, an online virtual pet site. I used their basic HTML and CSS guides to customize my pet page and eventually created personal blogs with more advanced coding such as PHP and Javascript on Wordpress. It wasn't until I took courses in college that I realized web development is my passion.
